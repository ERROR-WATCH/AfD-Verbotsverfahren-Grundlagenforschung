PRESSEBELEG (OFFLINE-REFERENZ): REUTERS
Thema: BfV categorizes AfD as an extremist threat case
Artikel: reuters.com_article_germany-security-agency-extremist-idUSKBN2XX0ZY

INHALT:
An official report by Reuters documenting that Germany's Federal Office for the Protection of the Constitution (BfV) has compiled thousands of pages of intelligence proving that multiple state chapters of the AfD as well as the federal youth wing "JA" meet the criteria to be officially categorized as right-wing extremist organizations.
