## Summary

This archive page presents translated excerpts from a 1937 Nazi school guide on heredity and racial science. The material argues that humans are divided into unequal races, frames heredity as a political principle, promotes selection and the elimination of hereditary illness, and treats blood purity and race mixing as central political concerns.

The page is directly relevant for the repository because it documents classic Nazi biologist and medicalized political language: people are sorted into valuable and less valuable groups, hereditary illness is treated as a burden, sterilization is framed as a state necessity, and race mixing is presented as decline.
