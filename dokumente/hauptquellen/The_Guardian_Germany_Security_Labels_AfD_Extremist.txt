PRESSEBELEG (OFFLINE-REFERENZ): THE GUARDIAN
Thema: Germany's domestic intelligence agency designates AfD as right-wing extremist
Artikel: theguardian.com_world_2025_may_03_germany-security-service-labels-afd-extremist

INHALT:
The Guardian's coverage on the historical escalation where Germany's domestic security agency designated the entire federal AfD party as an "active extremist group." This landmark move laid the groundwork for the current 2025/2026 Bundestag and Bundesrat discussions regarding a formal ban under Article 21 of the German Basic Law.
