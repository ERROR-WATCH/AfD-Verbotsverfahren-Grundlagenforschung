Summary
This article analyses Bjorn Hocke’s vocabulary and places it in the context
of National Socialist and fascist rhetoric. It highlights recurring terms and
motifs such as Tat-Elite, Bewegungspartei, Entartung, Degeneration, Dekadenz,
Verfall, Deutschland erwacht, tausendjaehrige Zukunft, Volksverderber, gesundes
Volksempfinden and disease metaphors around Keime and Feuchtbiotope.
The article is relevant for the repository because it treats Hocke’s language
not as isolated provocation, but as a structured vocabulary field with clear
historical continuities: the Volk appears as an organic body, decline is framed as
degeneration, and political struggle as awakening or healing.
